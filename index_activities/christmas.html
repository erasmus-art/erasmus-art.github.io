Making and writing Christmas cards,
sending them to partner schools

We introduce Christmas as a typical Christian tradition with historical roots.
Sending Christmas cards is a tradition in which relatives and acquaintances wish all the best for this holiday.
It is also a tradition that follows the pre-Christian holiday of the solstice, which was already celebrated by our ancestors in the period when the day begins to lengthen again and the night shortens.
