Easter Cards

Easter is a Christian holiday commemorating the crucifixion of Jesus. People send Easter cards to each other and wish them a happy Easter. The holiday is based on the pagan welcome of spring, which our ancestors did. It is also the spring festival.
We make cards and send them to eachother, wish happy Easter and spring.
