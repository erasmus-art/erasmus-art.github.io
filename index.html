Art is everywhere!

The project: “Art is everywhere” unites six schools from the Czech Republic, Bulgaria, Italy, Spain, Portugal and Turkey. The main coordinator of the project is the Czech school. The goal of the project is to work together on the subject of art. Our activities are varied: European cultural heritage, making decorations and cards for Christmas and Easter, celebrating Halloween and Valentine's Day, visiting theater and cinema and getting to know this kind of art, getting to know old crafts, art in cooking, famous artistic personalities , work with typical art software. Our work on this topic will give us a lot of knowledge about other cultures, teach us to respect the other, improve our knowledge and skills to communicate with others.

Partnering schools:

  • ZS a MS Tabor, Husova 1570 – Czech Republic

  • Escola Dr. Horácio Bento de Gouveia – Madeira, Portugal

  • Fundacion San Juan y San Pablo – Spain

  • 2° Istituto Comprensivo „G. Bianca“ – Avola(SR) – Italy
