Doll factory and museum

Paola Reina doll factory

The Paola Reina doll factory is situated in a town called 'Onil', which is about 15km from Ibi.

During our visit in this factory, the students were shown how plastic dolls are made - from pouring the plastic into the moulds, to painting the fingernails of the dolls.

At the end of the tour, everyone was given a present from the factory - a piggy bank !
