Trip to Valencia

Movie statues museum visit

During our stay at that interesting museum, we learned how movie props such as statues and other objects are used in the famous blockbusters.

We also saw different caricatures with a funny meaning with political, scientific and artistic meaning.

There were also famous paintings like 'Mona Lisa', but also containing funny elements - Mona Lisa with a Visa card, Mickey Mouse killing the Pink Panther and others.

Ciudad de las Artes y las Ciencias - Museum of science and art

The visit in the museum was outstanding!

The students were able to experiment and try out different physics laws in a practical way, which was quite memorable for them.

There main topics were physics, biology, chemistry and astrology.
