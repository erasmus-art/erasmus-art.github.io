Last day in Spain

Painting a wall in the school

In the last day of our lovely visit of Spain, all the groups were given different paint brushes and dyes. The students' job was to paint the Erasmus+ logo on one of the wall in the San Juan y San Pablo school.

The previous day, the lovely teacher Cynthia asked the students for their favourite music hits, which wwere played on a speaker during the painting of the logo.

The atmospehere was absolutely astonishing.

Orienteering around the city of Ibi

At the end of the day, the students were divided into different groups and then were given a map and instructuons on how to follow the said map.

The students would run around the city in groups and collect hidden points, marked on their maps.

It involved coordination and cooperation between the students from the differet countries, which helped them bond for one last time!
