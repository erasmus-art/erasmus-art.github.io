Shevitsa and Tsarevets monument

Shevitsa workshop

Before noon - Workshop: "Rhythm in Bulgarian embroidery". Everyone gets acquainted with the symbolism in Bulgarian embroidery and the rhythm in it.

Then they practice according to a certain pattern to make different motifs on their own and to compose music according to different embroidery motifs.

Tsarevets hill

In the afternoon, the participants in the working meeting visited Veliko Tarnovo and visited the Tsarevets hill.

In the evening they watched the light show: "Sound and Light" under Tsarevets Hill.
