Gifts to First Graders

Pupils make welcome gifts for future first graders in the Czech school, especially in Arts lessons. They are motivated to do their best because they want their gift to be chosen by them during the welcoming ceremony. First graders take the gifts home and it helps them to adapt to starting stool attendance.
