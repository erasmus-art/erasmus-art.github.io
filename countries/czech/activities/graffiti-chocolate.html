Graffiti and Chocolate

Graffiti, pottery and chocolate museum

The students were separated into two groups and each had some activities to do.

The first group painted some graffiti and the second one worked with pottery. They changed roles afterwards.

After lunch, we visited the Museum of chocolate, where a workshop about decorating and making chocolate figures was held.
