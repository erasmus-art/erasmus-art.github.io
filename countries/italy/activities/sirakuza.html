Sirakuza

Archeological park and museum

The day began with a visit to the Archaeological Park of Naples. In the papyrus museum we learned about the history of papyrus and the production of papyrus paper. The students made papyrus paper themselves during their participation in a workshop: Productive papyrus paper in one day.

In the afternoon we participated in a laboratory for making Sicilian dolls.
