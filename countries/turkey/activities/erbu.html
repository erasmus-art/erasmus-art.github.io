Erbu and Blue eye technique

Erbu and Nazarköy

Visit to the School of Fine Arts and acquaintance with the technique of Ebru - painting in water. All students make their own drawings using the Ebru technique.

In the afternoon we visited Nazarköy, a village where Blue Eye amulets are made. We got acquainted with the way the famous amulets are made to protect against bad eyes.
